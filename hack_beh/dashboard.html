📂 Dataset & Methodology
Data Source

Dataset: Cresci-2017 Social Spambots

Source: zenodo.org/record/573049

Paper: Cresci et al., "The Paradigm-Shift of Social Spambots", WWW 2017

Size: 5,827 accounts — 3,474 genuine + 2,353 bots (3 campaigns)

Features: 23 behavioral signals engineered from account metadata, tweet patterns, network structure, and linguistic signals

Intervention Strategies
High Risk (Bot Prob > 0.8)
Suspend account, investigate IP clusters, report coordinated campaign to platform trust & safety
Medium Risk (0.5–0.8)
Flag for manual review, reduce algorithmic amplification, require CAPTCHA/2FA verification
Low Risk (< 0.5)
Mark as genuine, continue monitoring with passive behavioral tracking, periodic re-evaluation
